notes: w=width, h=height, the value doesnt have to be exactly accurate

  1. Open XD
  2. Draw a perfect square, call it square1
  3. Draw a verticle rectangle (rec1) underneath the square, with its h = doubled the square h, and make its w only a bit bigger than the square's w. Make sure that the two shapes are centered
  4. Draw two more squares (square2,3) underneath the rectangle, with w/h = 1/2 square1
  5. Put them under the lower corners of rec1
  6. Add some space among the elements, don't make them stick toghether.
  7. Color all of these shapes the seagreen color
  8. Within square1, draw a face for it, draw 2 black square with w/h = 1/4 square1
  9. Make them square1's "eyes"
  10. Underneath the eyes, draw square1's mouth, consists of 1 horizontal black rectangle (w = eyes'w; h = 1/2 eyes'h)
  11. Draw the "corners of the mouth", draw two black square (w/h = 1/2 mouth's width), put them at both ends of the mouth, move them down so that the upper (left or right) corners of the "corners of the mouth" touch the lower (left or right) corners of the mouth
  12. You should be done, email it to me at hle49@gmu.edu
